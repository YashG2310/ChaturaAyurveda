Awla, Jatmansi, Brahmi, Khadir. Bakuchi, Sugandhi kachori, Jaswand, Bhringaraj/Maaka, Aloevera, Neem powder,Amla hair oil, Rosemary, Rosewood.
  1. Open the packet and pour the contents into a mixing bowl.
  2. Gradually add 400 to 500 ml of lukewarm water to the powder.
  3. Mix thoroughly until a smooth, consistent paste is formed.
  4. Allow the paste to sit for 5 to 6 hours to activate the ingredients.
  5. Apply the paste evenly to your scalp and hair, ensuring complete coverage.
  6. Leave the paste on for 60 to 70 minutes. Rinse thoroughly with warm water. Do not use shampoo.
  7. If your scalp feels dry after rinsing, apply a nourishing oil to your scalp, leave it on for 5-6 hours, and then shampoo as usual.

Caution

  • For external use only.
  • Conduct a patch test before full application to check for any allergic reactions.
  • Discontinue use if any irritation or discomfort occurs.
  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight.
  • Consult a dermatologist if you have sensitive skin or specific allergies.
